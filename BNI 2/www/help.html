Getting Started

Welcome to AccountedFor

Set up your group

First go to the Admininstration tab, and under General Settings you can name your group, set a meeting fee and a meeting time.

Next you will want to add members to your group. Under Members on the Administration tab, you can add their information. To make changes to saved members you can select them from the Existing Members drop down box and load their info for editing by pressing Edit Selected.

You can also choose a logo for your group with the Set Logo button on the Administration tab.

Start the meeting

Press the "Start Meeting" button on the Administration tab. The screen will switch to the Members tab and the members can begin to sign in as they arrive at the meeting. Members how have pre-paid their meeting fees can just press the "sign in" button next to their name, the meeting fee will be deducted from their balance and the time of their arrival will be recorded. Those who do not have a balance must press the "pay and sign in" button and record their payment.

After the meeting

After the meeting press the "Stop meeting" button. When you have an internet connection you can press the “send receipts” button and everyone who brought money at the last meeting will receive receipts by email. There is also a “send reports” button that will send and attendance report to the Vice President or whomever you choose to receive the report.
